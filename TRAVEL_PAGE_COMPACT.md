- Icons + labels

## User Flow (Minimal Clicks)

1. **Select Category** - 1 tap (Hotels/Flights/Cars/Tours)
2. **Enter Location** - Type or tap quick destination
3. **Set Guests** - Tap +/- buttons
4. **Search** - 1 tap

**Total: 3-4 taps to search!**
